Fastest path
Manage account in Outseta

Use the More menu in the header, then open Accounts to sign in to your Outseta customer account. That is where you update your subscription, billing details, and cancellation settings.

FAQ

How TrackIQ works

TrackIQ is the member login point for your subscription tier. Once you sign in, the system checks your Outseta membership and sends you to the correct TrackIQ page for Starter, Value, or Pro.

FAQ

How to read the tables

Start with the strongest-ranked runners and then compare the supporting data shown in the table. Use the table as a decision tool: review ranking, price context, and any supporting filters before deciding whether the race fits your approach.

FAQ

What each filter means

Filters narrow the race view to the profile you want to inspect. They are there to isolate the runners or race conditions that match the TrackIQ logic, so members can focus on specific patterns rather than scan every line equally.

FAQ

How to upgrade or downgrade

Open the More menu, click Accounts, sign in to Outseta, and open your customer billing area. From there you can change your active plan and confirm the updated subscription settings.

FAQ

How to cancel

Open the More menu, click Accounts, sign in to Outseta, and open your subscription settings. Use the cancellation option inside your customer account. If anything looks wrong, email Support before the next billing cycle.

FAQ

Contact and support

For login problems, billing questions, or member support, email Support@puntingiq.com.au or use the contact page for the right inbox.

Account guide

  • Login: open the More menu in the top navigation and choose Accounts to sign in through Outseta.
  • Plan changes: upgrade or downgrade from your customer billing area after login.
  • Cancellation: use the subscription controls in Outseta to cancel your plan.
  • Need help: if the account area does not show the expected plan or billing detail, email Support.

Need something else?

If your question is not covered here, contact the team directly. Support is the main customer-facing inbox and the contact page lists the other business email addresses.
