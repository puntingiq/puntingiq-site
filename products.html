Why PuntingIQ Is Different

Built on more than 25 years of racing data, not short-term opinion.

PuntingIQ is uniquely positioned in the way it approaches selections. Our process is built on more than 25 years of racing data and evaluates the variables that actually shape race outcomes: run style, barrier, track condition, distance profile, and the way those factors interact in each event. We then combine that with jockey and trainer performance patterns, using our own internally built Jockey, Trainer, and Tempo maps rather than relying on industry-standard ratings assembled by someone else. The result is not a generic tipping sheet. It is a disciplined intelligence process designed to find edges that most services do not even measure.
